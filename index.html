About College Event Manager

Our platform bridges the gap between event organizers and participants in college campuses. We provide a centralized system to manage all types of college events including technical fests, cultural programs, workshops, and seminars.

With features like online registration, payment integration, and automated notifications, we make event management seamless for both organizers and attendees.
